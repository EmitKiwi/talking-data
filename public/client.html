Info & instructions

Create your personal story on the map.

Waag Society invites you to tell your personal story and create beautiful data graphics that you can print on your t-shirt, dress or bag or cut-out in wood or plastic as a earring or necklace. How? By selecting the places that you share special memories with or that are either way important to you.
Add places by push and drag on the map, push and hold a circle to change the size. When your story is set on the map you can send it to produce it.
